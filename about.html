ABOUT

I was born in winter, 30th December 1983, Sempeter, Slovenia.

I started rewindig the movie of my life as I remember it. Thinking about what drew me to look at the world through the eyepiece of a camera. And the only thing that pops to my mind is that back in elementary school I was attracted to the covers of various metal bands. I admired the design that stands under those little plastic boxes, every time I bought a cassette or a CD.

And now, years after, I am a certified photographer, licenced from SAE Institute, but above all, I am an ARTIST. And everything I do, I do with my heart and my soul while striving to perfection.

My life motto is:

"IF YOU CAN LEARN TO CONTROL DARKNESS, THAN YOU WILL BE THE MASTER OF THE LIGHT."

With love DaLiloveart, since 2012.
